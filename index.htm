Pekingese

Pekingese are a Chinese ancient breed similar to a Pug. Pekingese are short snout like pug but have long thin hair and are usually smaller. Starting at $300 for males.

Best in Show 2021

Wasabi the Pekingese

Wasabi the Pekingese won best in show at the 2021 Westminster Dog Show. Wasabi hails from Pennsylvania, where his handler David Fitzpatrick lives. Starting at $2.00 / frame.
